Woman's Stats Project

In fact, 11 countries rank higher. Yes, the system clearly needs to improve and the obvious disparities are economic and gender bias. The heated debate in the United States especially when it comes to women's reproductive health rights is caused by lobbyists with outdated dangerous beliefs which is not uncommon all over the world.
